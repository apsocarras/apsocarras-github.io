About Me

I am a data engineering student at Epicodus, a 27-week full-time software development bootcamp and internship program that's partnered with Data Stack Academy to provide a comprehensive, cloud-centered DE curriculum. Prior to a shift in career goals, I was preparing for PhD programs in biostatistics and public health.

After realizing that building software (and breaking it) was more exciting to me than academic research, I decided Epicodus was the perfect springboard to leverage my professional experience with managing, processing, and analyzing data into a career in data engineering.
